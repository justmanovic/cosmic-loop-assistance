## About the Game

Cosmic Loop is an arcade survival game where your ship is trapped in the gravitational pull of a dying star. Collect energy crystals, dodge alien patrol ships, defeat bosses, and survive as long as possible.

## Frequently Asked Questions 

**Q: How do I save my progress?** 
A: Your high score is automatically saved on your device and synced to Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android).

**Q: The game is lagging. What can I do?**
A: Try closing other apps running in the background and restarting the game.

**Q: How do I access the leaderboard?** 
A: Tap the "Leaderboard" button on the main menu. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android). 

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?** 
A: Please open an issue on this repository or contact us via email below.
